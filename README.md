## 📖 About ScholarVault

If you are tired of constantly switching between a generic note-taking app, a subscription-based PDF scanner, a budget tracker, a flashcard maker, and a scientific calculator, you have come to the right place. 

ScholarVault is designed from the ground up to be the only productivity application a student truly needs. Whether you are a high school student juggling assignments, an undergraduate managing a tight budget, or a researcher organizing hundreds of PDFs, ScholarVault provides a unified, deeply integrated, and 100% offline-first environment.

---

## 🌟 Why ScholarVault?

1. **The "All-in-One" Promise Executed Right**
The evidence suggests that most "all-in-one" apps end up functioning as a clunky mess of web wrappers. ScholarVault is built natively for Android utilizing **Jetpack Compose**, offering a declarative, silky-smooth, battery-efficient, and beautifully cohesive Material Design 3 experience. The internal tools interact seamlessly without latency.

2. **100% Offline-First & Privacy Respected**
Academic data is intensely personal. Your financial budgets, class notes, and private schedules should not be mined for advertising. ScholarVault requires **no internet access**, **no account creation**, and utilizes zero hidden telemetry. All data is securely stored locally on your device via Room SQLite databases.

3. **Zero Cloud Lock-in**
We do not automatically transmit your notes or wallet logs to external servers. You maintain absolute sovereignty over your data.

## 🛠️ Core Features

### 📚 Academic Organization & Active Recall
*   **Course & Semester Manager:** Catalog your academic terms, input course details, and track assignments seamlessly.
*   **Grade Tracker:** Break down grading rubrics (e.g., Quizzes 20%, Midterms 30%) and track your cumulative GPA trajectories effortlessly.
*   **Quick Notes & Rich Formatting:** Create structured, rich-text lecture notes with minimal friction.
*   **Integrated Flashcards:** Convert your notes directly into flashcard decks, utilizing spaced repetition strategies to prepare for rigorous final examinations.
*   **Smart Push Notifications:** Configure custom deadline reminders to ensure an assignment never catches you off guard.

### 📂 Pro-Level PDF & Document Hub
*   **Native PDF Reader:** Provides blazing-fast rendering. Search text, zoom smoothly, and manage dense documents locally.
*   **Dark Mode PDF Inverter:** Mitigate eye strain during late-night study sessions. The algorithm intelligently flips document colors while preserving image clarity.
*   **PDF N-Up Processing:** Conserve resources at the campus print shop by condensing 2, 4, or 6 slides onto a single digital page prior to printing.
*   **Built-in Hardware Scanner:** Snap a photograph of a whiteboard or examination paper; the system auto-detects edges and corrects perspective natively. Export directly to standardized PDF formats.

### ⏱️ The "Lifesaver" Micro-Tools
*   **Floating Calculator:** Renders a movable computational window over your PDFs or notes. Calculate equations while actively analyzing the problem.
*   **Image Resizer & Compressor:** Quickly compress assignment photographs to meet strict university portal constraints (e.g., "Must be under 2MB") without degrading visual legibility.
*   **Focus Timer (Pomodoro):** Lock into a deep-work timer to cultivate discipline directly within the application environment.
*   **Audio Dictation:** Capture audio memos or record complex lectures effortlessly.

### 💰 Student Wallet & Budgeting
*   **Offline Ledger:** Maintain a detailed record of academic and personal expenditures (e.g., textbooks, tuition, supplies).
*   **Pattern-Locked Security:** Apply a custom, encrypted pattern-lock barrier to your financial or sensitive modules to secure private data from unauthorized access.

## 👩‍🎓 A Day in the Life with ScholarVault

**Consider a demanding daily workflow within higher education:**

1. **8:00 AM:** You sit in a seminar. You start an *Audio Recording* to capture the lecture while simultaneously drafting *Quick Notes*.
2. **11:00 AM:** An assignment is due. You utilize the *Scanner* to photograph your handwritten mathematical proof, combine the images via the *Image to PDF Builder*, and reduce the file size utilizing the *Compressor*.
3. **1:00 PM:** While studying for a midterm, you open a 120-page textbook PDF. You deploy the *Floating Calculator* over the text to verify practice problems and subsequently jump to your *Flashcards* deck to reinforce theoretical concepts.
4. **5:00 PM:** You purchase coffee, instantly logging the expenditure into your *Wallet* under the "Food" category to maintain your weekly financial parameters.

**The Result:** Complete mitigation of context switching. All required tasks are executed securely and efficiently within a singular, unified platform.
